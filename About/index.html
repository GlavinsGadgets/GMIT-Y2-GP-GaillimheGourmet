About Our Resturaunt

Located on shop street and near most central attractions in Galway, Gaillimhe Gourmet is the ideal restaurant for a casual lunch or dinner, or a special celebration.
Our restaurant is located in the heart of the medieval centre of Galway in the historical Latin Quarter, which still exhibits a strong presence of 16th and 17th century architecture.
