Gaillimhe Gourmet

Wellcome to the site! Feel free to look around an check out our menu.

Gaillimhe Gourmet is an Galway inspired resturaunt set up in the heart of the city of Galway, Ireland. We believe in quality and serve only the best food. If we had an album we'd call it "Now Thats What I Call Food"

We offer modern Irish food prepared with quality fresh local produce. Our speciality is seasonal seafood, but diners have plenty of choice, from meat to vegetarian dishes. With two floors, which have been recently refurbished, Gaillimhe Gourmet provides the perfect atmosphere for any occasion

Feeling lazy? Feel free to use our order online ferture to have our food delivered dirrectly to your door.
